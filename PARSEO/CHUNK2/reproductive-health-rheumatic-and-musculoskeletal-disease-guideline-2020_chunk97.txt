We conditionally recommend discontinuation of NSAIDs pre-conception if the patient is having difficulty conceiving (and if disease control would not be compromised), due to the possibility of NSAID-induced unruptured follicle syndrome, a cause of subfertility We strongly recommend against use of NSAIDs in the third trimester because of the risk of premature closure of the ductus arteriosus We conditionally recommend nonselective NSAIDs over cyclooxygenase 2-specific inhibitors in the first 2
