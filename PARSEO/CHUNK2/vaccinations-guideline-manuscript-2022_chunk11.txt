Pneumococcal vaccination For patients with RMD age <65 years who are taking immunosuppressive medication, pneumococcal vaccination is strongly recommended. Patients with RMDs taking immunosuppressive medication may be at increased risk of pneumococcal infection There are few studies evaluating the impact of diseasemodifying antirheumatic drugs (DMARDs) on conjugate pneumococcal vaccines.
