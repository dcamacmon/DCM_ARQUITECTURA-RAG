<h1>When to administer live-attenuated rotavirus vaccination to infants exposed to immunosuppressive medications in utero</h1>

<p>|Antenatal drug exposure in second or third trimester|Within the first 6 months of life|After 6 months of life|
|---|---|---|
|TNF inhibitor|Give rotavirus vaccine| |
|Rituximab|Do not give rotavirus vaccine|Give rotavirus vaccine|</p>
