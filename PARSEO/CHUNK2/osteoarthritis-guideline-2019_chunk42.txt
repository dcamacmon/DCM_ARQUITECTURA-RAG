The currently available literature does not demonstrate clear efficacy of lateral or medial wedged insoles. Acupuncture is conditionally recommended for patients with knee, hip, and/or hand OA. Although a large number of trials have addressed the use of acupuncture for OA, its efficacy remains a subject of controversy.
