We conditionally recommend against HRT use in patients with APS who are receiving anticoagulation treatment and in patients with APS who are currently negative for aPL. We conditionally recommend consideration of HRT, if desired, in women who have a history of positive aPL but are currently testing negative for aPL and have no history of clinical APS.
