RENAL REPLACEMENT THERAPIES: GPS: Decisions for initiation and type of dialysis and timing for kidney transplant require close collaboration with nephrology. In people with LN and ESKD, we strongly recommend kidney transplantation over dialysis. In people with LN who have progressive loss of kidney function and are nearing ESKD (eGFR of 15 ml/min/1.73m 2 ), we conditionally recommend preemptive kidney transplant over dialysis
