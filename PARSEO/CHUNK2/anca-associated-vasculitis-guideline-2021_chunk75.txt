position statement: The duration of glucocorticoid therapy in EGPA should be guided by the patient's clinical condition, values, and preferences. There is insufficient published evidence to support a specific duration of glucocorticoid treatment, and thus, the length of glucocorticoid therapy should be determined based on each patient's clinical circumstances. Many patients with EGPA require some treatment with glucocorticoids, generally at a low dose, to maintain control of asthma and allergy
