Methotrexate and azathioprine are equally efficacious for remission maintenance Recommendation: For patients with severe GPA/MPA whose disease has entered remission after treatment with cyclophosphamide or rituximab, we conditionally recommend treatment with methotrexate or azathioprine over leflunomide for remission maintenance. Methotrexate or azathioprine treatment is recommended over leflunomide due to the data supporting and clinical experience using methotrexate
