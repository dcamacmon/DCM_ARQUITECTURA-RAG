Two very small studies have suggested analgesic benefit of colchicine in OA, but the quality of the data was low. In addition, potential adverse effects, as well as drug interactions, may occur with use of colchicine. Fish oil is conditionally recommended against in patients with knee, hip, and/or hand OA.
