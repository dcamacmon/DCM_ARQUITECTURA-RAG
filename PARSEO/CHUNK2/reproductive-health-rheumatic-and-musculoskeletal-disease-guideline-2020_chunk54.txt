In addition, normal pregnancy symptoms such as malar erythema, chloasma gravidarum, anemia, elevated erythrocyte sedimentation, and diffuse arthralgias may falsely mimic symptoms of active RMD. Pregnancy-induced hypertension syndromes (preeclampsia) may be confused with lupus nephritis, scleroderma renal crisis, or vasculitis flare.
