Diet recommendations (Table 5) We conditionally recommend adherence to a Mediterranean-style diet over no formally defined diet. The Mediterranean-style diet pattern emphasizes the intake of vegetables, fruits, whole grains, nuts, seeds, and olive oil and the intake of moderate amounts of low-fat dairy and fish, and limits the use of added sugars, sodium, highly processed foods, refined carbohydrates, and saturated fats.
