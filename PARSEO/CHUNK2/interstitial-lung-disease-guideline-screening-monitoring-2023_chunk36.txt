RA-ILD typically progresses more slowly than the other SARDs; however, a usual interstitial pneumonia (UIP) pattern confers a worse prognosis and may warrant more frequent monitoring. Monitoring those with RA-ILD with PFTs every 6 to 12 months is suggested, recognizing that specific situations may warrant more frequent monitoring (3-6 months).
