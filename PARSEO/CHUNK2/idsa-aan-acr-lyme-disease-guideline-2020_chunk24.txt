VI. What are the preferred antibiotic regimens for the treatment of erythema migrans? Recommendation: 1. For patients with erythema migrans, we recommend using oral antibiotic therapy with doxycycline, amoxicillin, or cefuroxime axetil (strong recommendation; moderate-quality evidence).
