For children and youth ages 4 to 17 years treated with GCs for >3 months who are at low or moderate risk for fracture, optimization of age-appropriate dietary and supplemental calcium and vitamin D to fulfill the Recommended Daily Allowance is conditionally recommended in addition to an exercise program.
