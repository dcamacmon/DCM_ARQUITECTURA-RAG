Optimal duration of prophylactic LMWH for asymptomatic aPL-positive patients undergoing ovarian stimulation has not been studied; this is a decision best made in consultation with the reproductive endocrinology and infertility specialist. The treatment is often continued until estrogen levels return to near-physiologic levels if no pregnancy occurs. Patients with OB APS will continue therapy throughout pregnancy.
